About Us

B.K. Birla Night College Green Club

EcoCampus is the official digital platform of the Green Club. We are a student-led initiative dedicated to fostering environmental consciousness and sustainable practices within our college campus and beyond.

Our Mission

To transform our campus into a zero-waste zone by integrating technology with environmental action.

Our Vision

A student community that is actively aware, responsible, and engaged in preserving biodiversity.

General & Account

Who can use this app?

EcoCampus is exclusively for registered students of B.K. Birla Night College. You must have a valid Student ID to log in.

My Student ID shows "Invalid".

Please ensure you are entering the 7-digit ID found on your ID card. If the issue persists, contact the college admin office or email support.

EcoPoints & Rewards

How do I earn points?

1. Daily Check-in: Open the app daily (+5 pts).
2. Recycling: Scan your QR at the plastic collection desk.
3. Events: RSVP and attend Green Club events.
4. Challenges: Upload photos for weekly eco-challenges.

How do I redeem a reward?

Go to the Store tab, select an item, and confirm purchase. Then, go to the Orders tab to find your QR code. Show this code at the canteen or store counter.

I lost my Check-in Streak!

Don't worry! You can restore a broken streak by paying a 50 EcoPoint penalty. Just tap the "Daily Check-in" card on the dashboard to see the option.

Technical Issues

Camera not working for challenges.

Please ensure you have granted "Camera Permissions" to your browser. If it still fails, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

AQI is not updating.

The Air Quality Index requires Location Permissions (GPS) to be active. Ensure your device location is turned on.
